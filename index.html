About Us

NyarkVid GidMans Ltd is a reputable and diversified company based in Kronom, Kumasi, committed to providing high-quality products and services across various industries. Founded and led by Managing Director, Gideon Nyarko, we specialize in the supply of Timber, Stones, Agro chemicals as well as General Trading and Agriculture.

Our Services

NyarkVid GidMans Ltd offers a broad range of services across several key sectors. Below are the services provided in each area:

1. Timber

NyarkVid GidMans Ltd supplies high-quality timber for various construction and furniture needs. The company offers a wide range of timber products, including hardwoods and softwoods, sourced from sustainable forests. These materials are ideal for residential, commercial, and industrial projects, ensuring durability and aesthetic appeal.

2. Stones

The company deals in natural stones, including building stones, decorative stones, and aggregates. These stones are used in construction, landscaping, and road projects, offering a sturdy, long-lasting solution. NyarkVid GidMans Ltd ensures that its stone products meet the highest quality standards for durability and appearance.

3. General Agro

At NyarkVid GidMans Ltd, we are dedicated to providing top-quality agro products directly from trusted farms and suppliers. Whether you’re a farmer, wholesaler, or consumer, we offer a wide range of agricultural products to meet your needs.

4. General Trading

As part of its general trading services, NyarkVid GidMans Ltd provides a variety of goods across different sectors, from household items to industrial products. The company acts as an intermediary for businesses and individuals, sourcing and distributing goods efficiently and at competitive prices

5. Agriculture

NyarkVid GidMans Ltd is also involved in agriculture, providing agricultural products and services such as seeds, fertilizers, and farming equipment. The company supports farmers with quality inputs to enhance productivity and contribute to the agricultural sector’s growth. Additionally, it may offer consultancy and solutions to optimize agricultural practices.
